Frequently Asked Questions

How does per-record pricing work?

We quote a fixed price per record based on the services you need and your volume. You'll know the total cost before we start. No hourly billing, no surprise charges.

What's the minimum project size?

Our minimum project is $2,000. This covers data cleaning, enrichment, or discovery for a dataset or market segment. Most clients start here and scale up as they see results.

How do volume discounts work?

Per-record rates decrease as your volume increases. Clients on monthly engagements processing larger datasets get the best rates. We'll walk you through the tiers when we scope your project.

Do you require an annual contract?

No. You can start with a single project and scale from there. Monthly clients can pause or cancel anytime. We earn your business every month.

How fast do I get results?

Most projects deliver in hours, not days. Large datasets or multi-market discovery may take longer. We'll give you a timeline with your quote.
