ICP Analysis

Stop guessing who your best customers are. We use AI to analyze your data and find the segments with highest LTV, best win rates, and lowest churn.

ICP analysis (Ideal Customer Profile analysis) is a data-driven method of identifying which customer segments generate the highest lifetime value, best win rates, and lowest churn for a business. Instead of building target profiles on gut feeling, ICP analysis examines actual closed-won and closed-lost deals across dimensions like company size, industry, tech stack, team structure, and buying behavior. The output is a prioritized list of segments with specific, measurable criteria that sales and marketing teams can target.

The Problem with Most ICPs

Most Ideal Customer Profiles are built on intuition. Someone in a conference room says "we sell to mid-market SaaS companies" and that becomes the target. But intuition doesn't answer the questions that matter:

  • Which segments have the highest lifetime value?
  • Where do we have the best win rates?
  • Which customers churn fastest (and why)?
  • Are we targeting segments we can't actually serve well?
  • Where should we focus limited marketing budget?

Without data, you're optimizing for the wrong customers—wasting budget on segments that churn and ignoring segments that would stick.
