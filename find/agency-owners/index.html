Who Sells to Agencies

Agencies are attractive customers. They buy software, outsource production, need operational support, and influence their clients' purchasing decisions. If you can get to agency owners, you often get multiplied reach.

Software vendors. Project management, time tracking, creative tools, analytics platforms, client reporting. Agencies are early adopters and will try new tools.

White-label services. SEO fulfillment, paid media management, content production, development, design. Agencies resell these to clients and need reliable partners.

Business services. Accounting, HR, legal, insurance. Agency owners need the same professional services as other business owners.

Training and consulting. Agency operations, pricing strategy, sales training, leadership development. Many agency owners want to grow but aren't sure how.

Technology partners. Hosting, security, development frameworks. Agencies make technology recommendations to clients, so getting agency adoption means client referrals.

Media and advertising. Publishers, ad networks, sponsorship opportunities. Agencies control media budgets and need inventory options.

Data Fields Available

  • Agency information. Name, address, phone, website, years in business, estimated headcount and revenue.
  • Owner/principal contacts. Founders, CEOs, managing partners. Direct email and phone when available.
  • Agency specialty. Full-service, digital, creative, PR, SEO, social, content, branding, development.
  • Industry focus. B2B, B2C, healthcare, tech, hospitality, nonprofit, financial services. Agencies often specialize.
  • Client indicators. Named clients when public, typical client size, retainer vs project focus.
  • Technology stack. Marketing platforms used, CMS preferences, analytics tools (when identifiable).

Common Questions

Marketing, digital, creative, PR, SEO, social, content, branding, development. Filter by specialty, size, industry focus, or geography.

Can you filter by industries they serve?

Yes. Healthcare agencies, B2B tech specialists, fintech-focused, hospitality experts. Many agencies specialize by vertical.

How current is the data?

Agencies change frequently. We verify fresh for each project rather than pulling stale databases. Built-to-order means current information.
