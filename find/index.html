Frequently Asked Questions

How do you build custom prospect lists?

We combine multiple data sources including business registrations, professional directories, industry databases, and web scraping. Each record is verified and enriched with contact information before delivery.

What information is included in the lists?

Standard lists include business name, address, phone, website, and owner/decision-maker contact info with verified email. Additional data points like revenue, employee count, and specialty can be added based on availability for the industry.

Can you target specific geographic areas?

Yes. We can build lists by country, state, metro area, county, city, or even zip code radius. Local targeting is especially useful for service businesses, franchises, and regional sales teams.
