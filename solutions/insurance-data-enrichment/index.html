The Insurance Data Gap Problem

You're selling to insurance agents, brokers, and agencies. You know your target markets and producer profiles. But your CRM has names and maybe agency phone numbers. That's not enough to reach busy producers who are constantly in the field.

Insurance is a massive, fragmented industry. Over 2 million licensed producers across thousands of agencies, MGAs, and carriers. Most agents work as independent contractors with limited online presence. Your SDRs are spending hours researching each prospect to find contact information that works.

Contact data is hard to find

Agents don't have corporate email addresses. Agency phone numbers go to front desks. Personal cell phones are guarded carefully. Without verified direct contact data, you're playing phone tag instead of talking to producers.

License data is incomplete

Is this producer actually licensed? What lines are they authorized to sell? Is their license current? These regulatory data points are critical for compliance but rarely attached to contact records.

Production data is invisible

You want to target top producers, not part-time agents. But premium data is locked in carrier systems. You can't tell the $5M producer from the $50K one without enriched signals.

Carrier relationships are unknown

Which carriers does this agent represent? Are they captive or independent? Without appointment data, you can't identify competitive displacement opportunities or target agents by carrier relationship.
