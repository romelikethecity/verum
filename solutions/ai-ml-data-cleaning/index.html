The AI Company Data Problem

Your engineering team builds sophisticated ML pipelines. Your data scientists obsess over training data quality. But your sales and marketing data? It's chaos.

The CRM has grown organically over funding rounds. Leads from conferences, demo requests, investor intros, product signups. Nobody standardized anything. Now your SDRs spend more time figuring out if a lead is real than actually selling.

This is the irony nobody talks about at AI companies.

Duplicates multiply with every data source

Same prospect, five different records. One from a conference scan. One from a demo request. One from an investor intro. One from LinkedIn outreach. One from a product signup. Your SDRs don't know which is current. Your sequences hit the same person multiple times. Your pipeline reports are fiction.

Technical buyers change jobs constantly

The ML engineers and data scientists you're targeting are in high demand. They change jobs frequently. That Director of ML you've been nurturing? Left three months ago. But your CRM still shows them at the old company. Your outbound keeps hitting dead ends.

Startup data is inherently messy

You're selling to startups and enterprises. Startups get acquired, pivot, run out of funding, or explode in growth. Your target account list from last quarter is already stale. Companies that fit your ICP six months ago might not exist anymore.

Event and conference leads are the worst

Badge scans from AI conferences. Business cards hastily entered. Webinar registrations with typos. Demo requests with personal emails. This data comes in messy and stays messy. It sits in your CRM polluting your targeting until someone cleans it.

You can't run effective outbound with dirty data. Your personalization fails when job titles are wrong. Your account-based motions break when company data is stale. Your SDRs lose trust in the CRM and start keeping their own spreadsheets.

Common Questions About AI Company Data Cleaning

How long does data cleaning take?

Most projects complete in 24-48 hours. Larger datasets or complex requirements might take a few days. We'll give you a realistic timeline before we start.

What types of data do you clean?

CRM exports, prospect lists, customer databases, event leads, investor contacts, and any B2B data your go-to-market team uses. We focus on sales and marketing data—the stuff that drives revenue.

How is this different from data enrichment?

Data cleaning fixes what you have—removing duplicates, standardizing formats, validating emails. Data enrichment adds new information like company size, funding stage, or tech stack. Most AI companies need both, and we offer both services.

What CRMs and tools do you work with?

Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive, Outreach, Apollo, and any system that can export data. If you can get it into a CSV or spreadsheet, we can clean it.

How do you handle sensitive data?

Encrypted transfers, secure processing, no data sharing with third parties, deletion after project completion. Standard security practices that AI companies expect.

Can you clean data from multiple sources?

Yes. We often receive exports from CRM, marketing automation, and sales engagement tools simultaneously. We can clean and deduplicate across all sources, giving you one unified clean dataset.
