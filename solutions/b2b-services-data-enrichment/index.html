The B2B Services Lead Data Problem

You're selling consulting, agency services, or professional expertise. Your CRM has company names and maybe a general contact. That's not enough to win competitive services engagements.

B2B services sales are relationship-driven. You need to reach the right executive with the right message at the right time. But decision-maker data is hard to find. Buying triggers are invisible. Your BD team spends hours researching each prospect before they can even start the conversation.

Decision-makers are hard to identify

Who buys your type of service at this company? Is it the CMO, the VP of Marketing, or the procurement team? Service buying patterns vary by company size and industry. Without knowing the right entry point, you're guessing who to contact.

Contact information is hidden

Executives protect their time. Direct phone numbers aren't published. Personal assistants screen emails. Your BD team sends messages to info@ addresses and hopes for the best. Without direct contact data, you're not breaking through.

Timing is invisible

Companies hire agencies and consultants when something changes. New leadership. Funding rounds. Reorganizations. Strategic shifts. But these triggers aren't in your CRM. You're reaching out randomly instead of when companies actually need help.

Competitive landscape is unclear

Who's their current agency? What consultancies have they worked with? Understanding incumbent relationships helps you position appropriately or identify displacement opportunities. But this intelligence takes hours to gather manually.
