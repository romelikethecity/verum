Frequently Asked Questions

What use cases does Verum support?

Verum supports a wide range of B2B data use cases including lead scoring, ABM targeting, CRM migration, data integration, sales prospecting, territory planning, ICP development, customer segmentation, market sizing, and more. We clean, enrich, and analyze data to support any revenue operations objective.

How do I know which use case applies to me?

Start with your business problem: Are leads not converting? Try lead scoring or lead qualification. Planning a CRM migration? Start with CRM migration cleanup. Launching ABM? You need ABM targeting data. If you're unsure, contact us for a free assessment and we'll recommend the right approach.

Can Verum handle multiple use cases at once?

Yes. Many clients combine use cases - for example, cleaning data before a CRM migration while also enriching it for better lead scoring. We scope projects based on your complete needs, not individual services.
