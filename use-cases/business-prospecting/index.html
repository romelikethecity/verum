Business Prospecting

Business prospecting is only as good as the data behind it. If your prospect list has wrong emails, outdated titles, and missing phone numbers, your outreach is wasted effort. We build and clean prospect lists so every record is accurate, complete, and ready for outreach.

Your sales team spends 30-40% of their time researching prospects instead of selling. They're Googling company info, hunting for direct dials on LinkedIn, and guessing at email formats. That research time should be selling time.

How We Build Better Prospect Lists

  • Target criteria definition. Tell us your ICP: industry, company size, geography, titles. We build a custom list matching your exact specifications.
  • Multi-source discovery. We pull from 50+ data sources to find prospects, cross-referencing to verify accuracy. Not just one database with one set of stale data.
  • Contact verification. Every email gets deliverability-tested. Every phone number gets validated. Titles get confirmed against current sources. Bad data never reaches your CRM.
  • Enrichment. Each prospect record includes firmographic context — company size, revenue, industry, tech stack — so your reps can personalize outreach without additional research.
  • CRM-ready delivery. Records are formatted for your CRM's import requirements. No cleanup needed on your end.

Prospecting Outcomes

  • SDRs who spend time on calls and emails instead of research because every prospect is already complete
  • Email deliverability above 95% because every address has been verified before outreach
  • Higher connect rates on cold calls because phone numbers are validated direct dials, not main lines
  • Faster ramp time for new reps because they start with ready-to-work prospect data from day one
  • Better pipeline quality because prospects are verified fits before anyone picks up the phone

Common Questions

How is this different from buying a list from ZoomInfo or Apollo?

Database providers give you self-serve access to their existing records. We build custom lists for your specific criteria, verify every record, and deliver enriched data that's ready to load. The accuracy is higher because we're verifying at the time of delivery, not serving cached records.

How many prospects can you deliver?

It depends on your criteria. Broad criteria like 'marketing managers at US companies with 50+ employees' can produce tens of thousands of prospects. Niche criteria like 'CTO at Series B fintech companies in Austin' will produce a smaller, highly targeted list. We'll tell you the estimated volume before starting.

What's the turnaround time?

Most prospect lists are delivered within 3-5 business days. Large lists (over 25,000 records) or highly specific criteria may take longer. Rush delivery is available for teams with urgent outreach needs.
