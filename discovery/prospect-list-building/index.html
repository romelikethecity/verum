Define Your Criteria

We can filter on virtually any criteria. Common ones include:

  • Geography. Country, state, metro area, zip code, radius from a point. As specific as you need.
  • Industry. NAICS, SIC, or plain English descriptions. Broad categories or specific niches.
  • Company size. Employee count, revenue range, or both. Exclude too small or too big.
  • Technology usage. Companies using specific software, platforms, or tech stacks.
  • Business type. B2B vs B2C, private vs public, standalone vs franchise.
  • Role/Title. Specific contacts at these companies. VP Marketing, IT Director, Owner.
  • Exclusions. Companies you already work with, competitors, specific accounts to avoid.

What You Get

Every list includes:

  • Company information. Name, address, website, phone, industry classification.
  • Firmographic data. Employee count, revenue range, year founded, company type.
  • Validation status. Every email and phone verified before delivery.
  • Key contacts. Names, titles, verified email, direct phone where available.
  • LinkedIn profiles. For contacts, enabling connection requests and research.

How It Works

Step 1: Define your criteria. We have a scoping conversation about exactly what you need. Industry, size, geography, roles, any special requirements.

Step 2: We build the list. Using multiple data sources to find companies matching your criteria. Quality over quantity.

Step 3: Validation pass. Every email verified, every phone checked, every business confirmed as operating.

Step 4: Enrichment pass. Fill in missing firmographic data, add contact information, standardize formats.

Step 5: Delivery. Clean file ready to load into your CRM, with documentation on what's included.

Common Questions

How is this different from buying a list?

Three ways: (1) Criteria—we build to your exact specs, not pre-defined categories. (2) Validation—every record checked before delivery. (3) Freshness—we build at project time, not sell stale inventory.

What if the list isn't what I expected?

We review criteria carefully before building and deliver a sample first for review. If something's off, we fix it before final delivery.

Can you exclude companies I already have?

Yes. Send us your current database and we'll exclude matches. You only get net-new prospects.

How long does list building take?

Simple lists: 3-5 business days. Complex lists with multiple criteria: 1-2 weeks. Rush available for urgent needs.
