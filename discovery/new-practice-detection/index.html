How We Detect New Practices

  • NPI Registry monitoring. New NPIs registered, practice location changes, new group practice formations. Official federal data.
  • Medical board license tracking. New physician licenses, specialty certifications, practice authorizations. State-level data.
  • Google Places scanning. New medical practices create Google listings early. We scan major metros weekly.
  • Healthcare directory monitoring. Healthgrades, Vitals, WebMD profiles. New listings indicate new practices.
  • Insurance network updates. Practices added to insurance networks signal they're open for business.

Common Questions

How new are the practices?

We catch most practices within 2-6 weeks of opening. NPI Registry is the fastest source. Some practices take longer to appear in all sources.

Do you differentiate new practices from relocations?

Yes. We flag practices that are genuinely new vs. existing practices that moved locations. Both can be valuable, but for different reasons.

Can you detect practices joining hospital systems?

Yes. We track independent practices that get acquired by hospital systems. Important if you sell differently to each.

What about virtual-only practices?

We can include or exclude telehealth-only practices based on your needs. Some products need physical locations; some don't.
