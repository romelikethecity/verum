Account-Based Contact Discovery

This isn't list buying. You're not getting generic contacts at random companies. You're getting specific people at specific companies you've already identified as targets.

You tell us:

  • Your target account list
  • Roles you need (VP Sales, IT Director, CFO, etc.)
  • Any contacts to exclude (already in CRM)

We find:

  • Contacts matching those roles at those accounts
  • Verified email addresses
  • Direct phone numbers where available
  • LinkedIn profiles for context

What We Deliver

  • Full name. First name, last name, properly formatted.
  • Current title. Verified as of discovery date. Titles change; we check.
  • Email address. Work email, validated for deliverability.
  • Phone number. Direct dial when available. Mobile numbers where appropriate.
  • LinkedIn profile. URL for context and connection requests.
  • Seniority level. IC, Manager, Director, VP, C-level. For prioritization.

  • SMB (under 100 employees): 40-60% coverage

Email accuracy: 90%+ on delivered addresses. Phone accuracy: 80%+ on direct dials.

We're transparent about what we can find. If a contact doesn't exist in sources, we don't make it up.

Common Questions

How is this different from ZoomInfo or Apollo?

Data platforms give you their existing database. We do custom research for your specific accounts. If the contact isn't in databases, we still try to find them.

What if you can't find contacts at an account?

You only pay for contacts we find. If an account has no discoverable contacts, there's no charge for that account.

Can you find specific people by name?

Yes, if you know who you're looking for. We can research specific individuals or research by role—your choice.

How current is the data?

We research at the time of your project. For ongoing needs, consider our Ongoing Maintenance to keep contacts current.
