Common Questions

How is this different from buying a list?

Lists are a starting point. Market expansion research is complete market mapping plus analysis. You get the list, but also understand the market structure, competition, and prioritization.

How long does research take?

Depends on scope. A single metro market map might take 1-2 weeks. A multi-region vertical expansion could take 4-6 weeks. We'll give you a timeline during scoping.

Can you research international markets?

Yes, though coverage varies by country. US, Canada, UK, and Western Europe have the best data availability. Other markets are possible but may have limitations.

What if I already have partial data?

Great. We can gap-fill around what you have. Send us your existing data and we'll identify what's missing.
