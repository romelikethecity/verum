Marketing Ops Data Problems We Solve

  • Email deliverability. High bounce rates, spam complaints, declining open rates. We validate emails and remove bad addresses before they hurt your sender reputation.
  • Missing segmentation data. Can't segment by industry, company size, or role because the data isn't there. We enrich records with the fields you need.
  • Lead scoring inaccuracy. Scores don't reflect reality because the data feeding the model is incomplete. We fill the gaps so scoring works.
  • ABM account gaps. Running account-based campaigns but missing contact coverage, company data, or account hierarchies. We complete the picture.
  • Marketing contact bloat. Paying for contacts who bounced years ago, never engage, or are completely invalid. We identify who to archive.
  • Duplicate contacts. Same person appears 3 times, splitting engagement history and confusing nurture tracks. We merge and prevent duplicates.

What We Do for Marketing Ops

Email list hygiene. Validate every email address. Remove invalids, catch-alls, and role addresses. Get bounce rates under control before your domain gets blacklisted.

Marketing contact audit. Identify contacts that shouldn't be marketing contacts—bounces, unengaged, invalid data. Stop paying for worthless records.

Segmentation data enrichment. Fill the fields that drive segmentation—industry, company size, job function, seniority. Campaigns hit the right people.

Lead scoring data fix. Enrich the firmographic and demographic data that feeds your scoring model. Scores that actually predict conversion.

ABM data completion. For target accounts, we ensure you have complete contact coverage, accurate company data, and proper account hierarchies.

Ongoing list maintenance. Weekly email validation, monthly enrichment passes. Your database stays healthy without constant attention.
