Sales Ops Data Problems We Solve

  • Lead routing failures. Leads going to wrong reps because of bad company data, wrong industry codes, or missing location info. We fix the data so routing works.
  • Territory misalignment. Accounts in wrong territories because addresses are wrong, HQs are confused with branches, or company hierarchies are unknown. We standardize and enrich.
  • Duplicate accounts and contacts. Same company appears 4 times with 4 different names. Same contact on 3 records. We find, merge, and prevent duplicates.
  • Bad phone and email data. Reps calling dead numbers, emails bouncing. We validate everything so reps connect on first try.
  • Missing firmographic data. Can't route, score, or tier accounts without employee count, revenue, industry. We fill the gaps.
  • Stale records. Contacts who left, companies that closed, data that's 3 years old. We validate and update so your database reflects reality.
