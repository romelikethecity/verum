Sales Ops worries about their data. Marketing Ops worries about theirs. But you? You have to make all of it work together.

The same customer appears differently in marketing, sales, and customer success systems. Duplicates split the customer journey. Attribution doesn't work because the data doesn't connect. Your single source of truth is actually three conflicting sources of confusion.

RevOps Data Problems We Solve

  • Fragmented customer records. Same customer exists differently across systems. We unify records into a single source of truth.
  • Cross-system duplicates. Duplicates within systems and across systems. We identify and resolve them with proper merge logic.
  • Broken attribution. Can't attribute revenue to marketing because data doesn't connect from lead to opportunity. We fix the linkages.
  • Missing lifecycle data. Gaps in the customer journey because data fields aren't populated or standardized. We fill and standardize.
  • Reporting inconsistency. Marketing reports one number, sales reports another, finance reports a third. We normalize the data so reports align.
  • Data governance gaps. No consistent rules for how data enters and flows through systems. We help establish and enforce standards.
