Why I Built Verum

I watched sales teams waste millions on bad data. Reps calling disconnected numbers. Emails bouncing. Duplicates everywhere. Companies paying agencies $12,000 and waiting 6 weeks for work that should take days.

The data services industry has been overcharging and underdelivering for too long. Their model was simple: bill by the hour, work slowly, scope creep every project into a five-figure engagement. No incentive to work faster. Slower meant more revenue.

That model is dead, and I killed it with AI.

Verum uses AI agents purpose-built for data cleaning, enrichment, and analysis. They understand contact data, company hierarchies, job title normalization, and the dozens of other problems that plague CRM databases. What used to take weeks now takes hours. What used to cost $10,000+ now costs hundreds.
