What We Check

  • USPS verification. Does this address exist in the postal database? Is it a valid, deliverable location?
  • Format standardization. Correct abbreviations, proper casing, USPS-standard formatting. "Street" becomes "St", "Avenue" becomes "Ave".
  • Component parsing. Break addresses into components: street number, street name, unit, city, state, ZIP. Catch missing pieces.
  • ZIP+4 completion. Append the 4-digit extension for more precise delivery. Often required for bulk mail discounts.
  • Residential vs commercial. Is this a business address or residential? Important for B2B targeting.
  • Vacancy detection. Is this address currently occupied? Catch vacant properties before mailing.

Common Address Problems We Fix

Incomplete addresses. Missing apartment numbers, suite designations, or directional prefixes. "123 Main" vs "123 N Main St Ste 200".

Misspellings. "Philladelphia" → "Philadelphia". "Callifornia" → "California". Common errors that cause delivery failures.

Wrong ZIP codes. City and ZIP don't match. Street doesn't exist in that ZIP. Address got corrupted somewhere.

Outdated information. Street names change. ZIP codes get reassigned. Cities annex new areas. Old data doesn't deliver.

Suite and unit issues. Missing suite numbers for commercial buildings. Wrong unit designations for multi-tenant addresses.

Common Questions

Do you validate international addresses?

We specialize in US addresses with USPS verification. Canadian addresses are supported. Other international addresses have limited validation.

Can you add ZIP+4?

Yes. We append ZIP+4 when available, which is required for many bulk mail discounts and improves delivery precision.

What about PO Boxes?

PO Boxes are validated like any other address. We verify the box exists at that post office.

Do you validate rural routes?

Yes. Rural routes and highway contract routes are validated against USPS databases.
