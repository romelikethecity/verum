  • Do-Not-Call registry check. Is this number on the federal DNC list? Essential for compliance.

Why Line Type Matters

Mobile numbers require TCPA consent for automated calls and texts. Calling without consent is a compliance risk. Knowing which numbers are mobile helps you stay compliant.

Landline numbers are typically business lines. Good for cold calling, but can't receive texts.

VoIP numbers often indicate tech-savvy prospects or remote workers. Sometimes harder to reach, but often good prospects.

Toll-free numbers are usually company main lines, not direct contacts. Rarely worth cold calling.
