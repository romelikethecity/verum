The CRM Quality Gate

Every piece of data that enters your CRM either helps you or hurts you. Good data drives sales. Bad data wastes time, damages reputation, and pollutes your reports.

Most companies have no quality gate. Data flows in from trade shows, purchased lists, partner integrations, web forms. Nobody checks it. The problems show up later: bounced emails, disconnected phones, duplicate records, wasted outreach.

Pre-import validation is your quality gate. Before data enters your CRM, we check it. Everything that matters, all at once. What passes the gate goes in clean. What fails gets fixed or excluded.

What We Check

  • Email validation. DNS, MX, SMTP verification. Catch bounces before they happen.
  • Phone validation. Format, active status, line type. Know which numbers work.
  • Business status. Is the company still operating? Catch closures before you call.
  • Address validation. Standardize and verify. Catch bad addresses before direct mail.
  • Duplicate detection. Compare against your existing database. Don't create more duplicates.
  • Data completeness. Which records have enough information to be useful?
  • Format standardization. Consistent formats, proper casing, normalized values.
