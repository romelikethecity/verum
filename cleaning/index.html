Frequently Asked Questions

What does data cleaning include?

Data cleaning includes deduplication, email validation, phone formatting, address standardization, name parsing, job title normalization, and removal of invalid or incomplete records. The specific services depend on your data quality issues.

How long does data cleaning take?

Most projects complete in 24-48 hours. Larger databases (100K+ records) or complex deduplication scenarios may take 3-5 days. We provide a specific timeline after reviewing your data.

Will you delete records from my CRM?

We don't delete anything without your approval. We flag records for review and provide recommendations. You decide what gets deleted, merged, or updated. We can handle the updates for you or provide a file for your team.
