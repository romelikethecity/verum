Common Questions About CRM Data Cleaning

How long does crm data cleaning take?

Most projects complete in 24-48 hours for databases under 100,000 records. Larger databases may take 3-5 business days. We'll give you an exact timeline after reviewing your data.

Do we need to export our CRM data?

For most CRMs, yes. You export a CSV or provide API access, and we work with the data file. For Salesforce and HubSpot, we can connect directly if you prefer. We'll discuss the best approach during the discovery call.

How do we re-import the cleaned data?

We provide the cleaned data in the same format you gave us (CSV, XLSX, etc.) with clear field mappings. We can also provide import guidance specific to your CRM. Some clients ask us to handle the import directly.
