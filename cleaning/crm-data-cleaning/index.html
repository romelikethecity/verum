The CRM Data Problem

Every CRM starts clean. Then reality happens.

Reps enter data inconsistently. Lists get imported without deduplication. Contacts change jobs but records don't get updated. Integrations create duplicate records. Marketing and sales enter the same person from different sources. Five years later, your CRM is a mess.

The symptoms are everywhere. Reps waste time researching contacts that don't exist anymore. Marketing emails bounce. Reporting is unreliable because the same account appears multiple times. Lead routing breaks because territory assignments don't match inconsistent data.

Validity research shows workers spend 13+ hours per week dealing with CRM data quality issues. That's a quarter of someone's capacity lost to bad data.

What We Clean

  • Duplicate detection and merge. Find duplicates by email, name + company, phone. Merge records keeping the best data from each.
  • Email validation. Identify bounces, spam traps, and invalid addresses. Flag or remove before they hurt deliverability.
  • Contact verification. Check if people still work where your CRM says. Flag job changes and departed contacts.
  • Field standardization. Consistent formats for names, companies, phones, addresses. No more "Corp" vs "Corporation" variations.
  • Data completeness. Identify records missing critical fields. Prioritize for enrichment or removal.
  • Picklist cleanup. Standardize industry, state, country, and other picklist values to your allowed options.
