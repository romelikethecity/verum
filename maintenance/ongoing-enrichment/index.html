One-Time Enrichment Isn't Enough

You enriched your database. It was a good project. Fill rates improved. But that was six months ago.

Since then, your database has grown. New leads came in incomplete. New accounts got added without firmographic data. That contact you couldn't find a phone number for? Maybe it's available now. But you're not checking.

One-time enrichment is a snapshot. Ongoing enrichment is continuous improvement. Every month, we attempt to fill gaps that couldn't be filled before. New data sources come online. Companies update their information. What was unfindable becomes findable.

What Ongoing Enrichment Fills

  • Contact information. Email addresses, phone numbers, LinkedIn profiles. Re-attempt missing data monthly.
  • Firmographic data. Employee count, revenue, industry classification, company type. Fill gaps and update stale values.
  • Technographic data. Tech stack, tools used, platforms. New installations get detected.
  • Company details. Headquarters location, founding year, social profiles, description.
  • Job details. Updated titles, seniority level, department, reporting structure.

How It Works

Initial gap analysis. We assess your database to understand what's missing. Which fields are most incomplete? Which records have the biggest gaps?

Monthly enrichment passes. Every month, we attempt to fill gaps using 50+ data sources. New records get enriched immediately. Existing incomplete records get re-attempted.

Source cycling. Different data sources have different coverage. We cycle through sources, so data unavailable from one might be found through another.

Quality validation. New data gets validated before delivery. No point filling a gap with bad data.

Why Ongoing Beats One-Time

New records need enrichment too. Your database isn't static. New leads and accounts arrive constantly. Ongoing enrichment catches them immediately.

Data availability changes. A contact who wasn't findable last month might be findable this month. Companies publish more information. People join LinkedIn. Databases update.

Sources improve. Data providers add coverage constantly. A company not in ZoomInfo might be in Apollo. What wasn't available anywhere might be available somewhere now.

Cumulative improvement. Each pass fills some gaps. Over time, fill rates compound. Records get steadily more complete.

Common Questions

What if a field is already filled?

We don't overwrite existing data unless you want us to. Ongoing enrichment focuses on filling gaps, not replacing what you have.

Can you update stale data too?

Yes, if you want. Some clients want us to refresh data over 12 months old even if it's filled. Others only want gap-filling. Your choice.

What data sources do you use?

50+ sources including major providers (ZoomInfo, Clearbit, Apollo) plus specialized sources for healthcare, technology, and other verticals. We match records against multiple sources.

How do you handle conflicting data?

We use confidence scoring and recency weighting. When sources conflict, we either pick the highest-confidence value or flag it for your review.
