What Competitor Analysis Involves

We combine your internal competitive data with external intelligence to build a complete picture of the competitive landscape.

Win/loss rates by competitor. When you face Competitor A, what happens? Competitor B? Under what conditions? Your CRM has this data but nobody's connected it.

Differentiation validation. What do you actually win on? Where do customers choose you vs competitors? Does this match what you think your differentiation is?

Competitor positioning analysis. How do competitors position themselves? What claims do they make? Where is there messaging overlap vs differentiation?

Market segment mapping. Which segments does each competitor focus on? Where is competition intense? Where are there gaps?

Pricing and packaging intelligence. How do competitors price? What packaging do they use? Where is there pricing leverage?

The Process

Step 1: Data collection (2-3 days). CRM competitive data, customer feedback, win/loss notes. Plus external intelligence gathering.

Step 2: Competitor research (3-4 days). Deep dive on key competitors: positioning, pricing, team, technology, customer base.

Step 3: Win rate analysis (3-4 days). Analyze competitive deals from your CRM. Find patterns by competitor, segment, deal type.

Step 4: Synthesis (2-3 days). Combine internal and external data. Build competitor profiles and strategic recommendations.

Step 5: Delivery and enablement. Present findings, create sales enablement materials, help with implementation.

Total timeline: 2-4 weeks depending on scope and number of competitors.
