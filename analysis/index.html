Frequently Asked Questions

What data do you need to run an analysis?

We typically work with your CRM export, customer list, or transaction data. The minimum is a list of companies or contacts. We can enrich sparse records with firmographic and technographic data before running the analysis.

How is this different from a BI dashboard?

BI dashboards show you what happened. Our analysis tells you what to do about it. We combine your internal data with external market data to identify patterns, segments, and opportunities that dashboards alone can't surface.

How long does an analysis take?

Most analyses are delivered within 1-2 weeks depending on data complexity. ICP analysis and customer segmentation are typically the fastest. Market sizing and competitive analysis may take longer if we need to build the dataset from scratch.
