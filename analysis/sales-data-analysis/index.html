What Sales Data Analysis Reveals

We pull your CRM data and look for patterns that explain performance. Not just what happened, but why it happened and what to do about it.

Rep performance drivers. What do top performers do differently? Territory quality? Activity patterns? Deal selection? Close technique? We find the differences that can be replicated.

Pipeline velocity analysis. How fast do deals move through stages? Where do they get stuck? What predicts a deal that will drag forever vs one that will close quickly?

Conversion rate analysis. Stage-to-stage conversion by rep, territory, deal type, source. Where does pipeline leak? Which stages are filter stages vs rubber stamps?

Activity correlation. Which activities actually correlate with closed deals? Calls? Emails? Meetings? The relationship isn't always what you'd assume.

Forecast accuracy analysis. Where do forecasts go wrong? Which stages are optimistic? Which reps over-commit? What deal characteristics predict slippage?
