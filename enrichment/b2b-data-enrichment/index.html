B2B Data Enrichment

B2B data enrichment appends firmographic, technographic, and contact-level data to your existing business records. We take what you have — often just a company name or domain — and return a complete profile with revenue, employee count, industry, technology stack, and decision-maker contacts.

Most B2B databases start strong and decay fast. Reps enter the minimum to log a deal. Marketing imports lists with just names and emails. Within a year, 30% of contacts change jobs and company data goes stale. Enrichment fills the gaps and refreshes what's outdated.

How We Enrich B2B Data

  • Company matching and identification. We match your records to our commercial databases using company name, domain, and fuzzy matching to find the right entity even with typos or abbreviations.
  • Firmographic append. We add revenue range, employee count, founding year, headquarters location, industry codes (NAICS/SIC), and ownership structure.
  • Technographic append. We identify the technology stack each company uses: CRM, marketing automation, analytics, e-commerce platform, and more.
  • Contact-level enrichment. For existing contacts, we verify and update job titles, direct phone numbers, and email addresses. For new contacts, we identify decision-makers by role.
  • Data quality scoring. Every enriched field gets a confidence score so you know which data points are verified and which are estimated.

What Enriched B2B Data Includes

  • Complete company profiles with revenue, headcount, industry, and location data
  • Technology stack detection showing which tools each company uses
  • Verified contact information for decision-makers including direct dials and emails
  • Confidence scores on every enriched field so you can trust the data
  • Duplicate flagging where multiple records match the same company

Common Questions

What's your typical match rate for B2B enrichment?

It depends on what you send us. If you have company domains, match rates are typically 70-85%. Company names alone match at 50-65% because of variations in how companies are named. Adding any second identifier (domain, phone, address) jumps match rates significantly. We report exact match rates for your specific file.

How is this different from buying a new list?

Enrichment builds on your existing data — your CRM contacts, your pipeline accounts, your customer list. A new list gives you new records but doesn't improve what you already have. Enrichment fills gaps in records you're already working, which means your reps and campaigns get better data on accounts they're already touching.

Can you enrich data in our CRM directly?

We work with exported files (CSV, Excel) and return enriched files in the same format. We don't connect directly to your CRM because direct API integrations risk overwriting data your team entered manually. You control what gets imported back and what doesn't.
