Frequently Asked Questions

What is data enrichment?

Data enrichment is the process of appending missing information to your existing records. If you have a company name but no employee count, industry, or revenue, enrichment fills those gaps using external data sources.

What data points can you enrich?

We enrich 40+ data points including email addresses, phone numbers, job titles, company size, industry, revenue, tech stack, funding history, social profiles, and more. Contact and company data can be enriched separately or together.

What's your typical enrichment match rate?

Match rates vary by data point and record quality. Email enrichment typically achieves 70-85% coverage for US B2B contacts. Firmographic data like company size and industry achieves 85-95% coverage for companies with an online presence.
