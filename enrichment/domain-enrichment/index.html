Domain Enrichment

Domain enrichment takes a list of website domains and returns complete company profiles: company name, revenue, employee count, industry, technology stack, headquarters location, and social media presence. It's the fastest way to turn anonymous website traffic or email sign-ups into identified, enriched accounts.

When someone signs up with jane@acmecorp.com, you have a domain. That domain is a key to unlock everything about the company — if you have the right data sources. Without domain enrichment, your marketing team is staring at an email address and guessing who the company is.

How We Enrich from Domains

  • Domain-to-company matching. We match each domain to a company record in our database, handling redirects, subdomains, and companies with multiple domains.
  • Full firmographic append. Matched companies get the complete profile: name, revenue range, employee count, NAICS/SIC codes, founding year, and location.
  • Technology detection. We scan the domain to identify the company's technology stack: CRM, marketing automation, analytics, hosting, and more.
  • Social presence mapping. We link each domain to the company's LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook, and other social profiles.

What Domain Enrichment Returns

  • Company name, revenue range, and employee count for each domain
  • Industry classification and headquarters location
  • Technology stack detected from the website
  • Social media profiles linked to the domain
  • Match confidence score for each domain-to-company resolution

Common Questions

What about free email domains like gmail.com?

We filter out consumer email domains (Gmail, Yahoo, Outlook, etc.) since they don't map to a company. We can flag contacts using free email addresses so you can handle them separately — some companies deprioritize free email sign-ups, others ask for a business email on follow-up.

Can you handle domains that redirect to another domain?

Yes. We follow redirects to the final destination domain and match against that. This catches companies that have rebranded, acquired a new domain, or use a vanity URL that redirects to their main site.

What's the match rate for domain enrichment?

Typically 70-85% for B2B company domains. Consumer-focused businesses, very small businesses, and newly registered domains have lower match rates. We report the exact match rate for your specific list along with the unmatched domains so you can review them.
