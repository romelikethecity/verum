Email Finder

Our email finder service takes a person's name and company and returns their verified business email address. Unlike email guessing tools that generate a format and hope for the best, we verify every address at the mail server level before returning it. This means lower bounce rates and better deliverability for your outreach.

You found the right person on LinkedIn. You know their name and company. But LinkedIn InMail has a 15% response rate on a good day, and connection requests go ignored. You need their email address. Manual searching takes 5-10 minutes per contact and often fails. At scale, an email finder service is the only practical option.

How Our Email Finder Works

  • Input matching. You provide the person's name, company name or domain, and optionally their title. We use all available identifiers to find the right person.
  • Pattern-based discovery. We identify the email naming convention at their company and generate the most likely email address based on that pattern.
  • Database cross-reference. We check our multi-source database for known email addresses associated with that person and company combination.
  • Server-level verification. Every candidate email address is verified through SMTP validation to confirm it exists and can receive mail before we return it.

What You Get from Email Finder

  • Verified email address for each contact you submit
  • Confidence score based on verification method and source quality
  • Catch-all domain flag for domains where individual verification isn't possible
  • Alternative email suggestions when the primary address can't be verified

Common Questions

How is this different from email guessing tools?

Email guessing tools generate an address based on a pattern (first.last@company.com) and give it to you without verifying it exists. Our email finder verifies at the mail server level before returning results. The difference shows up in your bounce rate: guessing tools produce 15-25% bounces, verified results produce under 3%.
