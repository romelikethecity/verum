CRM Detection

CRM detection identifies which customer relationship management platform each company in your database uses: Salesforce, HubSpot, Microsoft Dynamics, Zoho, Pipedrive, or one of dozens of others. This technographic data point is critical for companies selling CRM integrations, add-ons, migration services, or competing CRM products.

If you sell a Salesforce integration, pitching a HubSpot shop wastes everyone's time. If you sell CRM migration services, you need to know who's on legacy platforms. Without CRM detection, you're guessing which prospects can actually use your product — and guessing wrong 60-70% of the time.

How We Detect CRM Platforms

  • Website technology scanning. We scan company websites for CRM-specific tracking scripts, form handlers, and API calls that reveal which platform they use.
  • Job posting analysis. Companies hiring for 'Salesforce Admin' or 'HubSpot Specialist' reveal their CRM choice in job descriptions.
  • Integration fingerprinting. We detect CRM-specific integrations with other tools (email, marketing automation, support) that confirm the platform.
  • Multi-source validation. We cross-reference technology detection with job postings and app marketplace data for higher confidence.

What CRM Detection Data Includes

  • CRM platform identified for each company (Salesforce, HubSpot, Dynamics, Zoho, Pipedrive, etc.)
  • Confidence level based on how many detection signals confirmed the finding
  • Edition/tier detection where possible (Salesforce Enterprise vs Professional, HubSpot Free vs Enterprise)
  • Complementary marketing automation platform detection (Marketo, Pardot, Mailchimp, etc.)

Common Questions

What CRM platforms can you detect?

We reliably detect Salesforce, HubSpot, Microsoft Dynamics 365, Zoho CRM, Pipedrive, Freshsales, Monday Sales CRM, Close, Copper, and most other web-based CRMs. On-premise CRMs like older Dynamics versions or Sugar are harder to detect from outside but can sometimes be inferred from job postings and integration patterns.

How accurate is CRM detection?

When we detect a CRM, accuracy is typically 85-90%. The main source of error is companies that have migrated recently — their old CRM's tracking scripts may still be on the website. We reduce this by combining multiple detection methods and flagging conflicting signals for review.

Can you tell us which CRM edition or tier a company uses?

Sometimes. Salesforce editions can often be distinguished by the features visible in their web presence. HubSpot's free vs paid tiers have different capabilities we can detect. But tier detection is less reliable than platform detection — we get it right about 60% of the time when we attempt it.
