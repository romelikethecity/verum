The Gatekeeper Problem

Cold calling works. But only when you actually reach the person you're calling. The main office number means navigating a phone tree, waiting on hold, getting transferred twice, and eventually landing in a voicemail box that nobody checks.

A direct dial changes everything. The phone rings on their desk. They pick up. You're talking. No gatekeepers, no waiting, no phone tag. Connect rates jump from 2-3% to 15-20% or higher. The same rep making the same number of calls gets five times more conversations.

Sales teams know this. That's why reps spend hours each week researching phone numbers before making calls. Checking LinkedIn, company websites, old business cards, anything to find a number that isn't the main switchboard. That research time is expensive.

A data protection company we worked with had their SDRs spending two hours per day on contact research before dialing. After we enriched their database with direct dials, that research time dropped to near zero. Their connect rates more than doubled because they were calling numbers that actually reached people.

What We Provide

  • Verified direct dials. Phone numbers confirmed to ring on your prospect's desk or phone, not the main switchboard. Verified as currently in service.
  • Mobile numbers. When available and appropriate. Mobile numbers often have higher connect rates than desk phones, especially for executives and remote workers.
