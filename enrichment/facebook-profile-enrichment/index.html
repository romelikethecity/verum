The Right Tool for Specific Jobs

Let's be direct: for most B2B prospecting, LinkedIn is the better platform. Executives, enterprise buyers, and technical decision-makers live there professionally. If that's your audience, start with LinkedIn enrichment.

But Facebook still matters for certain audiences. Real estate agents maintain business pages religiously. Local service providers build community through Facebook groups. Small business owners in traditional industries often have stronger Facebook presence than LinkedIn presence. D2C brand founders are active where their customers are.

If you're selling to these segments, Facebook profiles provide research context you can't get elsewhere. Their community engagement, customer interactions, how they present their business publicly. Different data than LinkedIn, and for the right use cases, more valuable.

What We Provide

  • Personal profile matching. Find the individual's Facebook presence when you need to understand them as a person, not just a professional.
  • Business page discovery. Find company pages for customer engagement analysis, content strategy research, and competitive intelligence.
  • Profile verification. We don't guess. Name, company, location cross-referencing to ensure correct matches. Confidence flags on ambiguous cases.
  • Activity indicators. Is this an active profile or dormant? Last post date, engagement levels. Helps you prioritize which profiles are worth researching.
  • No-match flagging. When someone doesn't have a findable Facebook presence, we tell you. No wasted time searching for profiles that don't exist.

How It Works

Step 1: Send us your contact list. Names, companies, and whatever else you have. Tell us if you need personal profiles, business pages, or both.

Step 2: We search and verify. Finding potential matches and cross-referencing against available signals.

Step 3: Human QA on matches. Common names and ambiguous cases get manual verification. We'd rather return "no match" than a wrong profile.

Step 4: Facebook data delivered. Verified profile URLs with confidence indicators. Ready for research and outreach planning.
