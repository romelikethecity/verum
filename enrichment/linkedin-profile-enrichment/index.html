The Research Problem

Every outreach works better when you know something about the person. Their career history. Their recent posts. Their connections. LinkedIn has all of this. But first you have to find the right profile.

For unique names, easy. For "Michael Johnson at Accenture"? There might be forty of them. Your rep searches, scrolls, tries to match title and location, and eventually picks one that looks right. Sometimes they get it wrong. Sometimes they give up and send a generic message.

This research time adds up. If your SDRs spend even 3 minutes per contact finding LinkedIn profiles, that's 2.5 hours per day at 50 contacts. Time they could spend actually talking to prospects.

What We Provide

How It Works

Step 1: Send us your contact list. Names, companies, and whatever else you have. Emails and titles help with matching common names.

Step 2: We find and verify profiles. Searching across LinkedIn and cross-referencing to ensure accurate matches. Common names get extra verification.

Step 3: Human QA on ambiguous matches. When multiple profiles could match, we verify manually. No guessing.

Step 4: Profiles delivered. Each contact with LinkedIn URL and any additional profile data you requested. Ready for research and outreach.
