The Stack Problem

You sell something that works with HubSpot. Or against Marketo. Or that replaces Pardot. Either way, the first qualification question is: what marketing automation are they using?

Without this data, you're guessing. Your reps ask on calls, which wastes time and sometimes gets wrong answers. Your campaigns go to everyone instead of the segment that matters. Your competitive positioning is generic instead of pointed.

Tech stack data changes the game. You can build campaigns specifically for "companies using Marketo with 500+ employees" or "HubSpot users in financial services." Your reps know before the call what they're walking into. Your messaging hits the actual pain points of their current solution.

What We Detect

  • Major marketing automation platforms. HubSpot, Marketo, Pardot (Salesforce Marketing Cloud), Eloqua, ActiveCampaign, Mailchimp, and more.
  • Enterprise marketing clouds. Salesforce Marketing Cloud, Adobe Experience Cloud, Oracle Marketing Cloud. The big enterprise stacks.
  • Email-focused platforms. Klaviyo, Braze, Iterable, Customer.io, Sendinblue, Constant Contact. Often used alongside a primary MAP.
  • Multi-platform environments. Many companies use multiple tools. We identify all detected platforms, not just the primary one.
  • No platform detected. Companies without detectable marketing automation get flagged. This is useful data too — they might be your best prospects.
  • Confidence levels. Clear indication of detection confidence. Strong signals vs. partial matches.

All major ones: HubSpot, Marketo, Pardot, Eloqua, ActiveCampaign, Mailchimp, Klaviyo, Braze, Iterable, Customer.io, and 50+ more.

How accurate is detection?

85-95% when platforms are actively deployed on the website. Some companies use MAP only for email, which is harder to detect. We note confidence levels.

Integration selling, competitive positioning, qualification. Knowing their stack lets you customize your pitch and prioritize better.
