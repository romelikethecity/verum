Twitter Profile Enrichment

Twitter/X profile enrichment appends verified Twitter handles to your contact and company records. For B2B sales, executive Twitter accounts are a source of talking points, engagement opportunities, and insight into a prospect's priorities and opinions that LinkedIn doesn't always surface.

Finding the right Twitter handle for a business contact is harder than it sounds. Common names have dozens of accounts. Many professionals use handles that don't include their name. Some have inactive accounts that haven't been used in years. Manual searching is unreliable at scale.

How We Match Twitter Profiles

  • Multi-signal matching. We match contacts to Twitter profiles using name, company, bio content, and linked websites to find the right account.
  • Verification and disambiguation. For common names, we use company and title context from your CRM to identify the correct profile among multiple candidates.
  • Activity assessment. We check recent posting activity to distinguish active users from dormant accounts, so your team engages where there's activity.
  • Company account matching. For company records, we find the official business Twitter account and distinguish it from personal accounts of employees.

What Twitter Enrichment Provides

  • Verified Twitter/X handle and profile URL for contacts and companies
  • Follower count and activity status (active, occasional, dormant)
  • Bio description for conversation context and personalization
  • Match confidence score for each profile assignment

Common Questions

What's the match rate for Twitter profiles?

For B2B executives and professionals, 30-50% match rates are typical. Twitter usage is lower than LinkedIn in the B2B world. Senior executives in tech, media, and consulting have higher match rates. Contacts in industries like manufacturing and healthcare use Twitter less frequently.

Do you pull tweet content or just the profile URL?

We return the profile URL, handle, follower count, bio, and activity status. We don't scrape individual tweet content. Your team can click through to the profile to read recent posts for conversation prep and engagement opportunities.

Has the Twitter/X rebrand affected your detection?

The platform transition from Twitter to X hasn't affected our matching methodology. Profile URLs and API access remain functional under both domains. We reference both names (Twitter/X) since many users and systems still use 'Twitter' as the common name.
