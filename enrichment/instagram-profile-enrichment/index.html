When Instagram Data Matters

Instagram isn't LinkedIn. It's not the default professional network for most industries. But for certain audiences, it's everything.

Creators and influencers. Fashion, beauty, design, and photography professionals. Real estate agents showcasing properties. Fitness instructors, wellness coaches, food and beverage brands. D2C founders who built their businesses on the platform. For these people, Instagram is their primary professional identity.

If you're building an influencer marketing platform, you need Instagram handles at scale. If you're doing creator outreach for brand partnerships, you need handles. If you're selling to visual industries where portfolio presence matters more than LinkedIn connections, you need handles.

We help you find them, verify them, and deliver them ready for outreach or platform integration.

What We Provide

  • Profile handle matching. Connect your contact records to their Instagram presence. Names, companies, and other signals triangulated to find the right account.
  • Creator vs brand separation. Personal creator accounts and brand business accounts clearly labeled. Different uses, different outreach approaches.
  • Profile verification. Cross-referencing against available signals to ensure you have the right person. Confidence flags on ambiguous matches.
