Social Media Enrichment

Social media enrichment appends social media profile URLs across multiple platforms to your company and contact records. We find and verify LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Facebook, Instagram, and YouTube profiles for both companies and individuals, giving your team the social context they need for research and outreach.

Your reps manually search LinkedIn before calls, check Twitter for talking points, and look up company Facebook pages for context. That's 5-10 minutes per prospect across multiple platforms. Social media enrichment automates this research step by pre-linking every record in your database to the right social profiles.

How We Find Social Profiles

  • Multi-platform search. We search LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Facebook, Instagram, and YouTube for profiles matching each company and contact in your database.
  • Identity verification. Each profile match is verified against your CRM data to ensure it's the right person or company, not a name match.
  • Company vs personal distinction. For individual contacts, we find personal profiles. For companies, we find official business pages and accounts.
  • Activity indicators. We flag whether each social account is active (posted recently) or dormant, so your team knows which channels are worth engaging.

What Social Media Enrichment Provides

  • Verified profile URLs for LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Facebook, Instagram, and YouTube
  • Account type classification (personal vs business) for each profile
  • Activity status (active, dormant, abandoned) based on recent posting
  • Follower/connection counts where publicly available

Common Questions

Which social platforms do you cover?

LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Facebook, Instagram, and YouTube are our standard platforms. LinkedIn has the highest match rates for B2B contacts. Twitter/X is useful for executives and industry thought leaders. Facebook and Instagram are most relevant for B2C and local businesses. We can add other platforms on request.

Do you match personal or company social profiles?

Both. For contact-level records, we match to personal profiles (individual LinkedIn, Twitter, etc.). For company-level records, we match to official business pages and accounts. You can request one or both depending on your use case.

What's the match rate across platforms?

LinkedIn has the highest match rate for B2B professionals (65-80%). Twitter/X matches at 30-50% because not all professionals use it. Facebook matches at 40-60% for companies (business pages) and lower for individuals. Instagram is strongest for B2C brands and consumer-facing businesses (50-70%).
