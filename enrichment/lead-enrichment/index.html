The Form Problem

Marketing spent a lot of money getting that person to your website. They read your content, decided you might solve their problem, and filled out a form. What did you capture? Name and email. Maybe company if you asked nicely.

Now what? Your SDR needs to research this person before calling. Who do they work for? What's their title? Are they even the right buyer? Is this a 50-person company or a 5,000-person company? They spend 10 minutes digging before they can make a single call.

Multiply that by every lead. If your SDR team handles 100 leads a week, that's over 16 hours of research time. Time they could be spending on actual conversations. And the research is often incomplete because manual googling only gets you so far.

Lead enrichment happens automatically. A form submission triggers enrichment. By the time the lead hits Salesforce, it's a complete record. Name, title, phone, LinkedIn, company, industry, size, technology stack. Your SDR picks up the phone and starts talking. No research delay.

What We Enrich

  • Contact data. Full name, job title, direct phone number, mobile when available, LinkedIn profile URL. Everything needed to reach the person.
  • Company identification. Company name, domain, headquarters location. Know exactly who they work for.
  • Firmographics. Industry, employee count, revenue range. Is this a fit for your ICP?
  • Technographics. What tools do they use? CRM, marketing automation, cloud infrastructure. Relevant for positioning.
  • Seniority and department. Are they a decision-maker? Which department owns this problem? Critical for routing and prioritization.
  • ICP scoring. Based on the enriched data, how well do they match your ideal customer profile?

How It Works

Step 1: Send us your leads. Form submissions, webinar registrations, content downloads. Whatever you're collecting.

Step 2: We match and enrich. Cross-referencing the email domain and any other signals against our databases to build complete profiles.

Step 3: Quality scoring. Each lead scored for completeness and ICP fit. You know which ones deserve immediate attention.

Step 4: Enriched leads delivered. Complete contact and company records ready for your CRM, scoring models, and SDR workflow.

Common Questions

What can you enrich from just an email?

Full name, job title, phone, LinkedIn, company name, industry, employee count, revenue range, headquarters, and technology stack. One email becomes a complete profile.

How fast can leads be enriched?

Batch enrichment in 24-48 hours. For real-time needs, we can set up integrations that enrich leads as they arrive.

What about personal email addresses?

Personal emails (Gmail, Yahoo) are harder since there's no company domain. We flag them so you know which leads need manual research.

Does enrichment improve conversion?

Yes. Faster scoring, better routing, more relevant outreach. SDRs spend less time researching and more time selling.
