What We Provide

  • Industry classification. NAICS codes, SIC codes, or custom taxonomies. Standardized so your filtering actually works. No more "Software" vs "Technology" vs "SaaS" inconsistency.
  • Employee count. Exact headcount when available, reliable bands when not. Updated regularly since companies grow and shrink faster than you'd expect.
  • Revenue estimates. Annual revenue ranges based on employee count, industry benchmarks, and available financial data. 80-90% accuracy on estimates.
  • Headquarters location. Full address with city, state, country. Distinguished from branch offices. Critical for territory assignment.
  • Company type. Public, private, nonprofit, government. Different types buy differently and have different budget cycles.
  • Corporate structure. Parent companies and subsidiaries mapped. Know who actually owns what before you pitch.
  • Year founded. Startups act differently than 50-year-old companies. Age often correlates with budget cycles and tech adoption patterns.
  • Social profiles. LinkedIn company page, Twitter handle, and other social presence. Additional context and research starting points.
