The Foundation Problem

You have a list of company names. Maybe from an event, a purchased list, or internal records that were never properly maintained. The company names are there. The domains aren't.

Without domains, you're stuck. Can't detect their technology stack. Can't derive email patterns. Can't verify they're a real, active business. Can't do much of anything except manual research, one company at a time.

Website enrichment solves this foundational problem. Match company names to their verified domains. Now you have something to work with. Now tech stack detection works. Now email pattern derivation works. Now you can actually enrich and research at scale.

What We Provide

  • Company-to-domain matching. "Acme Corporation" becomes "acme.com". Name variations, location context, and multiple signals combined for accurate matching.
  • Domain verification. We check that domains resolve, aren't parked or expired, and lead to active company websites.
  • Disambiguation handling. "Apple" in Cupertino is different from "Apple" in Iowa. We use all available context to find the right company.
  • Subsidiary and regional handling. Primary corporate domain by default. Subsidiaries, regional sites, and brand domains available on request.
  • Website status indicators. Active, redirecting, under construction. Know what you're getting.
  • No-match flagging. When we can't find a verified domain, we tell you clearly. No guessing.

Why Domains Unlock Everything

Domains are the key to company-level intelligence:

Technology detection. Can't scan a tech stack without a website to scan. Domains enable technographic enrichment.

Email patterns. firstname.lastname@domain.com. firstname@domain.com. Without the domain, you're guessing.

Company verification. A real website confirms you're dealing with a real company, not a typo or a business that closed.

Further enrichment. Many data providers key off domains. Website enrichment enables everything that comes after.
