The Context Problem

Your pitch changes based on their stack. If they're using a competitor, you position against it. If they're using something you integrate with, you lead with that. If they have no solution in your category, that's a different conversation entirely.

Without tech stack data, every conversation starts from scratch. Your reps ask discovery questions that could have been answered before the call. Your campaigns go to everyone instead of the segments most likely to buy. Your competitive intel is limited to what prospects volunteer.

Tech stack enrichment gives you this context at scale. Before you reach out, you know what they're running. Your messaging is relevant from the first touch.
