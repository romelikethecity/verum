Why Teams Look for ZoomInfo Alternatives

ZoomInfo is the 800-pound gorilla in B2B data. It's a powerful platform with a massive database. But that doesn't mean it's the right fit for every team. Here's what we hear most often from people exploring alternatives:

  • The cost is hard to justify. ZoomInfo contracts typically start at $15,000/year and climb quickly. For mid-market teams or companies that don't need unlimited access to 300M+ contacts, that's a lot of budget tied up in a single tool. And once you're in, the renewal conversation usually involves a higher number.
  • Data quality is inconsistent. A large database doesn't automatically mean an accurate one. Many teams report that 20-30% of the contacts they pull from ZoomInfo have outdated emails, wrong titles, or stale phone numbers. You end up spending time cleaning the data you just paid to access.
  • It's a self-serve platform that still requires staff. ZoomInfo gives you the tools, but someone on your team still needs to build lists, run exports, manage filters, and QA the output. If you don't have a dedicated RevOps or data ops person, the platform sits underutilized.
  • Overwhelming for smaller teams. The feature set is built for enterprise sales orgs. Workflows, intent data, conversation intelligence, website visitor tracking. If you just need clean, accurate contact data for your CRM, you're paying for a lot you won't touch.

None of this makes ZoomInfo a bad product. It's excellent at what it does. But if you're a team of 10-50 and you need someone to actually do the data work for you, there's a different approach worth considering.

Common Questions

Can Verum replace ZoomInfo completely?

It depends on how you use ZoomInfo. If you primarily use it to enrich and clean your existing CRM data, yes. If you rely on ZoomInfo's intent data, website visitor tracking, or conversation intelligence features, those are different products that Verum doesn't replace. Many teams use Verum for data quality and a lighter-weight tool (or nothing at all) for prospecting.

How does pricing compare?

ZoomInfo contracts typically start around $15,000/year for a small team and can exceed $50,000/year at scale. Verum charges per record processed, so a typical project might run a few hundred to a few thousand dollars depending on volume. There's no minimum commitment and no annual contract. You can do a single project and walk away.

What if I need both net-new contacts and data cleaning?

Verum does both. We can clean and enrich your existing database, and we can also build targeted contact lists for specific markets or ICPs. The difference is that we build those lists for you, verify them, and deliver them ready to load into your CRM. You're not filtering through a database yourself.
