Why teams switch from self-serve to managed

Self-serve data platforms promise a lot. You get a login, a credit balance, and access to a massive database. In theory, you can find any contact, enrich any record, build any list.

In practice, your team spends hours filtering, exporting, deduplicating, and cleaning data that still bounces at 15%. Someone has to manage the subscription, track credit usage, train new reps, and figure out why half the phone numbers go to voicemail. The platform becomes another tool to maintain instead of a problem it was supposed to solve.

Verum takes a different approach. You tell us what you need, we deliver clean, verified data. No logins to manage, no credits to burn through, no learning curve. Per-project pricing instead of annual contracts. And because we validate every record before delivery, you're not paying to clean up the data you just bought.
