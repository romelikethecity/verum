The Data Quality Difference

ZoomInfo has impressive database size—over 100 million business profiles. But size and accuracy aren't the same thing. Studies suggest that even premium B2B databases have 20-30% data decay annually.

More importantly, ZoomInfo gives you access to data. Verum actually cleans and enriches your specific data. The difference matters:

  • ZoomInfo approach: Search their database, export contacts, import to your CRM. Now you have new records alongside your existing duplicates and outdated data.
  • Verum approach: Export your CRM, we deduplicate and validate everything, enrich with missing fields, return clean data. Your entire database is better, not just bigger.
