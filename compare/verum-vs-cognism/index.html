The Honest Comparison

Cognism has carved out a strong position in the B2B data market by focusing on what others do poorly: European data and mobile phone numbers. Their GDPR-compliant approach and "Diamond Data" verified phone numbers make them popular with teams selling into EMEA.

But Cognism is fundamentally a prospecting platform. You search their database, build lists, and export contacts. They help you find new people to call.

Verum solves a different problem: making your existing data accurate. We clean duplicates, validate contacts, and enrich missing fields. No prospecting platform, no list building—just better data.

When Cognism Makes Sense

  • You're selling into Europe. Cognism's EMEA data coverage is stronger than most US-focused competitors. Their GDPR compliance gives legal comfort.
  • Phone outreach is critical. Cognism's Diamond Data program human-verifies phone numbers. If cold calling is core to your sales motion, that matters.
  • You need to build prospect lists. Cognism's platform lets you search by industry, company size, job title, and more to build targeted lists.
  • You want intent data. Cognism offers intent signals to identify companies researching relevant topics.

When Verum Makes Sense

  • Your existing data needs cleaning. Cognism adds new contacts; it doesn't fix duplicates and outdated records in your CRM. Verum does.
  • You don't need another prospecting platform. If you already have data sources, Verum makes them accurate without adding another tool.
  • Email is your primary channel. If phone outreach isn't central to your sales motion, Cognism's phone specialization matters less.
  • You want project-based work. Clean before a campaign, enrich before a push, maintain quarterly. No annual platform commitment.
  • You need deduplication. Verum specializes in finding and merging duplicate records. Cognism adds records without deduplicating against what you have.

The GDPR Question

Cognism markets heavily on GDPR compliance, and it's a legitimate strength for teams concerned about European privacy regulations. Their data sourcing and consent practices are designed with GDPR in mind.

Verum handles data you provide, so GDPR compliance depends on how you collected that data originally. We process securely and delete after project completion, but we're not a source of new contact data.

If GDPR-compliant prospecting is your priority, Cognism addresses that directly. If you're cleaning data you've already collected through compliant means, Verum helps without adding new compliance questions.

Yes. They address different problems:

  • Use Cognism to build EMEA prospect lists and get verified phone numbers

Cognism helps you find new European contacts. Verum helps you keep your entire database—including Cognism data—clean and accurate.

Common Questions

How does Verum compare to Cognism?

Cognism is a self-serve platform focused on GDPR-compliant European data with strong mobile phone coverage. Verum is a done-for-you data service that cleans and enriches your existing database. Cognism is for prospecting; Verum is for data hygiene.

Is Verum better for European data than Cognism?

For building new prospect lists in Europe, Cognism's GDPR-compliant database is stronger. For cleaning and enriching existing European data, Verum can help. Different problems, different solutions.

Does Verum offer phone number data like Cognism?

Verum's enrichment can include phone numbers from various sources, but we don't specialize in mobile phone data the way Cognism does. If direct dials and mobile numbers are your priority, Cognism focuses more heavily on that.

Which is better for cold calling?

Cognism. Their Diamond Data phone verification and focus on mobile numbers makes them the better choice for teams doing significant phone outreach.
