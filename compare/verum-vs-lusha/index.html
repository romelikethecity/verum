The Honest Comparison

Lusha made contact data simple: install a browser extension, browse LinkedIn or company websites, click to reveal email addresses and phone numbers. For individual prospectors who need quick access to contact details, it's convenient and affordable.

But Lusha is designed for one-at-a-time lookups. You find a person, get their contact info, move on. It doesn't help when you have thousands of existing records that need cleaning or enrichment.

Verum works at the database level. Export your CRM, we process everything—deduplicating, validating, enriching—and return clean data. Different scale, different approach.

When Lusha Makes Sense

  • You need quick, one-off lookups. Browsing LinkedIn and need an email? Lusha's extension makes it instant.
  • You're an individual contributor. For solo SDRs or small teams doing manual prospecting, Lusha's per-credit model works.
  • You want a free starting point. Lusha's free tier (5 credits/month) lets you try before committing.
  • You need phone numbers. Lusha includes direct dials and mobile numbers in their credits, which some competitors charge extra for.

When Verum Makes Sense

  • You have thousands of records to process. Lusha's credit model gets expensive at scale. Verum's project pricing is more economical for bulk work.
  • Your database needs cleaning. Lusha adds contact info; it doesn't deduplicate or fix existing data. Verum does both.
  • You want someone else to do the work. Lusha requires you to look up each contact. Verum processes your entire database while you focus on selling.
  • You need validation, not just enrichment. Verum validates existing contacts—checking if they've moved, emails still work, companies still exist. Lusha doesn't validate your current data.
  • You don't want another tool to manage. Verum delivers clean data back to your existing systems. No browser extension, no platform to learn.

The Scale Question

Lusha pricing makes sense for low-volume lookups:

  • Free: 5 credits/month
  • Pro: $36/user/month (480 credits/year)
  • Premium: $59/user/month (960 credits/year)
  • Scale: Custom pricing

At $36/month for 40 credits, you're paying roughly $0.90 per contact. That's reasonable for occasional lookups.

But if you need to enrich 5,000 records, that's $4,500 in Lusha credits—plus the time to look up each one manually. A Verum project handling 5,000 records might cost $2,000-3,000 and be done in 24-48 hours with no manual work on your end.

Can You Use Both?

Yes. They serve different purposes:

  • Use Lusha for quick lookups when you find interesting prospects on LinkedIn
  • Use Verum quarterly to clean your entire CRM, including records from Lusha and other sources

Lusha helps you add new contacts one at a time. Verum helps you keep your entire database—including Lusha data—accurate and deduplicated.

Common Questions

How does Verum compare to Lusha?

Lusha is a browser extension and platform for quick contact lookups—find someone on LinkedIn, get their email and phone. Verum is a done-for-you data service that cleans and enriches your entire database. Lusha is for one-off lookups; Verum is for database-wide improvements.

Is Verum cheaper than Lusha?

Depends on use case. Lusha's free tier gives you 5 credits/month. Paid plans start at $36/user/month. Verum charges per project, starting around $500. For individual lookups, Lusha is cheaper. For cleaning thousands of records, Verum is more cost-effective.

Does Verum have a browser extension like Lusha?

No. Verum works on batch data projects—you send us your database, we clean and enrich it, you get it back. We don't offer real-time lookups or browser extensions.

Can Verum clean data I got from Lusha?

Yes. We regularly clean databases that include data from Lusha, ZoomInfo, Apollo, and other sources. Part of our value is deduplicating across all your data sources.
