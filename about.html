The Industry We're Disrupting

For decades, data cleaning, enrichment, and analysis has been dominated by services companies and agencies. Their business model was simple: bill by the hour, work slowly, and scope creep every project into a five-figure engagement.

A "simple" data cleaning project would take 6 weeks and cost $12,000. Why? Because that's what 80 hours at $150/hour costs. And there was no incentive to work faster—slower meant more revenue.

We watched this happen over and over. Companies paid premium rates for contractors copy-pasting between spreadsheets, manually researching LinkedIn profiles, and making the same formatting mistakes at 4pm on Fridays that they'd catch on Monday morning.

That model is dead. We killed it with AI.

How We're Different

We built AI agents specifically designed for B2B data operations. Not generic AI tools—purpose-built systems that understand the nuances of contact data, company hierarchies, job title normalization, and the dozens of other problems that plague CRM databases.

These agents work 24/7. They don't get tired. They don't make Friday afternoon mistakes. They don't bill you extra for "scope changes" when your data is messier than expected.

The result: what used to take weeks now takes hours. What used to cost $10,000+ now costs hundreds. And the quality is better because AI processes every record with the same precision—no variance based on who's working on your project that day.

Why We Charge for Outcomes, Not Hours

The hourly billing model creates perverse incentives. When you bill by the hour, you're incentivized to work slowly, to find additional scope, to schedule unnecessary meetings.

We charge per record. You know exactly what you'll pay before we start. Our incentive is to deliver quality results fast—because that's how we make money and build reputation. Your win is our win.

This is only possible because of AI. Without AI, per-record pricing would mean losing money on complex projects. With AI, we can process 100,000 records with the same efficiency as 1,000.
